Master of Arts (MA) in Mass Communication

Course Description:
This interdisciplinary program covers a wide range of topics essential to drama and film studies. From exploring the history of theater and cinema to analyzing contemporary works and emerging trends, students will engage with a variety of texts, performances, and visual media to deepen their understanding of dramatic and cinematic expression. Through lectures, seminars, workshops, and practical projects, students will develop critical thinking skills, creative insights, and practical abilities relevant to both fields.

Duration: 3 years

Shedule: Weekends

Prize: LKR 365,000

Master of Science (MS) in Mass Communication

Course Description:
This interdisciplinary program covers a wide range of topics essential to mass communication scholarship and practice. From examining the socio-cultural impact of media to exploring media industries, policies, and regulations, students will engage with theoretical frameworks, research methodologies, and practical applications relevant to the field. Through seminars, research projects, internships, and professional development activities, students will develop the expertise and leadership abilities needed to address complex challenges and opportunities in mass communication.

Duration: 3 years

Shedule: Weekends

Prize: LKR 375,000
