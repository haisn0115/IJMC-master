Diploma in News and Feature Article Writing

Course Description:
This intensive program covers a wide range of topics essential to news and feature article writing. From mastering the fundamentals of journalism ethics and news reporting to exploring the art of feature writing and long-form storytelling, students will learn how to captivate audiences with engaging content. Through a combination of lectures, workshops, and hands-on assignments, students will develop their writing skills, cultivate their journalistic voice, and navigate the evolving media landscape with confidence.

Duration: 1 year

Prize: LKR 65,000

Shedule: Weekdays and Weekends

Diploma in Mass Media and Journalism

Course Description:
This intensive program covers a wide range of topics essential to the practice of journalism and communication. From media ethics and law to digital storytelling and multimedia production, students will explore the key principles and practices that underpin modern journalism. Through interactive lectures, workshops, and collaborative projects, students will gain a deep understanding of the role of media in society and learn how to navigate ethical and legal challenges in the industry.

Duration: 1 year

Shedule: Weekdays and Weekends

Prize: LKR 85,000

Through a combination of lectures, case studies, hands-on projects, and simulations, students will develop the skills and expertise necessary to excel in digital marketing roles.

Duration: 1 year

Shedule: Weekdays and Weekends

Diploma in Drama and Film Studying

Course Description:
This interdisciplinary program covers a wide range of topics essential to the study of drama and film. From exploring the history of theater and cinema to analyzing contemporary works and emerging trends, students will engage with a variety of texts, performances, and visual media to deepen their understanding of dramatic and cinematic expression. Through lectures, seminars, workshops, and screenings, students will develop critical thinking skills, creative insights, and practical abilities relevant to both fields.

Duration:1 year

Shedule: Weekdays and Weekends

Prize: LKR 85,000

Diploma in Voice Acting

Course Description:
This immersive program covers a wide range of topics essential to voice acting success. From mastering vocal techniques and character development to understanding script analysis and studio etiquette, students will learn the fundamentals of voice acting through a combination of theoretical study, practical exercises, and hands-on projects. Through voiceover workshops, recording sessions, and industry insights from experienced professionals, students will develop the confidence, versatility, and professionalism required to excel in the world of voice acting.

Duration: 1 year

Shedule: Weekdays and Weekends

Prize: LKR 75,000
