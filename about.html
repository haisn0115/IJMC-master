FAQ

  • What are the eligibility criteria for admission?

    • The eligibility criteria vary depending on the program. Generally, for undergraduate programs, candidates must have completed high school or equivalent. For postgraduate programs, a bachelor's degree in a relevant field is required. Specific requirements may also include entrance exams, interviews, and portfolio submissions.

  • How can I apply for admission?

    • You can apply for admission through our online application portal on our institute's website. Make sure to check the application deadlines and required documents before applying.

  • What programs does your institute offer?

    • Our institute offers a range of programs in journalism and mass communication including undergraduate degrees, postgraduate degrees, diplomas, and certificate courses.
