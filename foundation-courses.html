Foundation Courses

Foundation courses are the cornerstone of media and journalism education, providing essential knowledge, practical skills, and an ethical framework crucial for success in this dynamic industry. These courses are invaluable, especially for advanced-level students who may feel unqualified or underprepared. By offering a structured and supportive learning path, foundation courses empower students to build confidence in their abilities and excel in their studies. They cover critical areas such as media ethics, communication theories, and digital media, ensuring that students are well-prepared for the challenges of both academic progression and professional life.


Moreover, these courses lay the groundwork for higher education and a successful career in media and journalism, enabling students to become informed and responsible media professionals. By mastering the basics, students set themselves up for a future where they can innovate, lead, and make significant contributions to the media landscape. Foundation courses truly form the bedrock upon which the next generation of media leaders is built, making them indispensable for anyone passionate about shaping the future of media and journalism. This foundational education is not just a stepping stone but the solid ground from which students launch their careers.

Fundamentals of Mass Communication

Course Description:
This intensive program covers a wide range of topics essential to news and feature article writing. From mastering the fundamentals of journalism ethics and news reporting to exploring the art of feature writing and long-form storytelling, students will learn how to captivate audiences with engaging content. Through a combination of lectures, workshops, and hands-on assignments, students will develop their writing skills, cultivate their journalistic voice, and navigate the evolving media landscape with confidence.

Duration: 3 Months

Prize: LKR 30,000

Shedule: Weekdays and Weekends
